MEET YOUR DENTIST

Dr. Bijal B. Lashkari

B.D.S., M.D.S. (Ahmedabad) — Consultant Oral & Dental Surgeon

Dr. Bijal Lashkari is an experienced dental surgeon with over 23 years of clinical excellence in delivering comprehensive dental care. She completed her Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S.) and Master of Dental Surgery (M.D.S.) in Ahmedabad, and has built a reputation for providing ethical, patient-focused, and high-quality dental treatments.

Known for her gentle approach and attention to detail, Dr. Lashkari believes that every patient deserves a confident, healthy smile. She communicates fluently in English, Hindi, and Gujarati, ensuring patients of all backgrounds feel comfortable and understood.

From preventive dental care to advanced restorative and cosmetic procedures, she is committed to delivering personalized treatment plans using modern techniques in a hygienic and caring environment.
